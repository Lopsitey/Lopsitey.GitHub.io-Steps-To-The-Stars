Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd presents

Steps to the Stars

A single player puzzle game following the journey of two stars as they climb to return to the constellation from which they have fallen.

Wishlist on Steam Play the Prototype on Itch.io

Little star has lost his shine, relying on big star to guide them safely through the shadows. Using her strength together they push blocks, press buttons and clear paths journeying through hand-crafted puzzles together, in the hopes of returning to the heavens.

Coming soon - PC first, future platforms under review
