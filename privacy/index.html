6. Purposes for processing

Information may be processed to operate and improve the game, provide support, respond to enquiries, manage platform requirements, investigate bugs, maintain security, comply with legal obligations and understand release performance.

7. Sharing and service providers

Information may be shared with service providers only where needed to provide the game, run platform features, host the website, process support messages, comply with law or protect the rights and safety of players and the developer.

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect information handled by Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd. No internet or platform service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Retention

Information is kept only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including support, legal, accounting, security and platform obligations.

Players can contact Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd to request access, correction or deletion of information handled directly by the developer. Some information may need to be requested through the relevant platform provider if it is controlled by that platform.

Send requests to contact@steps-to-the-stars.co.uk.

Steps to the Stars is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. Age ratings, platform requirements and any child-directed obligations must be reviewed before release.
