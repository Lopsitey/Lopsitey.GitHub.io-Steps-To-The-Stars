Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd

A small UK indie studio developing Steps To The Stars, a single player puzzle game about two fallen stars finding their way back home.

Overview

A focused studio built around one careful climb

Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd was formed to develop Steps To The Stars from a student prototype into a commercial indie release with a clearer production path, platform presence and long-term support plan.

The studio is currently focused on production planning, public store materials, media, privacy documentation and the practical route from Itch.io prototype toward PC release and future platform exploration.

The goal is to make a compact, polished puzzle experience with handmade levels, readable mechanics and a gentle story about light, shadow and returning home.
