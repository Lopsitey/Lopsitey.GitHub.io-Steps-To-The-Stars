Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd

Indie development company for Steps to the Stars, built around creative experimentation, accessible design and sustainable production.

Overview

A small studio formed around one focused project.

Celestial Studios Interactive Ltd was formed to develop Steps to the Stars from a student prototype into a commercial indie release with a clearer production path, platform presence and long-term support plan.

The studio's current focus is the game's PC release preparation, including store assets, final feature work, public communications and the privacy documentation needed for wider platform planning.

Values

A practical creative compass.

Creative experimentation

Prototype quickly, keep the unusual ideas alive, and let the finished game keep some handmade character.

Accessible design

Plan options, controls and presentation with a wider range of players in mind, then test those claims before publishing them.

Sustainable indie development

Build scope carefully, communicate honestly and avoid promising features before they are actually shippable.
